# Social Media Marketing Plan for a Hybrid Graphic Designer & Web Developer

## 1. Purpose & Brand Positioning
- **North Star Goal:** Build a recognizable hybrid designer–developer brand that attracts high-value freelance clients, agency collaborations, and startup projects.
- **Value Promise:** End-to-end creative partner who blends Adobe Creative Cloud expertise, modern web development, and AI tooling to ship visually compelling, high-performing digital experiences.
- **Key Differentiators:**
  - Demonstrated mastery across design and code workflows (UI/UX, motion, front-end builds).
  - Early adoption of AI-assisted creativity (Adobe Firefly, Runway, Cursor, Claude/Gemini scripting).
  - Transparent process storytelling: sketch → prototype → production.

## 2. Audience Segments & Personas
1. **Visionary Founders & Startup Teams**
   - Need modern product sites, launch campaigns, quick iteration.
   - Motivated by speed, innovation, and flexible collaboration models.
2. **Creative & Digital Agencies**
   - Seek specialist partners for AI-enhanced design, interactive micro-sites, and overflow work.
   - Value proven systems, clean handoffs, and up-to-date toolchains.
3. **Small Business Owners / Personal Brands**
   - Want refreshed visual identity, conversion-focused landing pages, and consistent social assets.
   - Motivated by before/after transformations and testimonials.

> **Voice & Tone:** Confident, collaborative, and educational—invite clients behind the scenes while highlighting measurable results.

## 3. Objectives, KPIs & Data Inputs
### Primary Objectives
- Increase inbound project inquiries by 30% in 90 days.
- Grow cross-platform audience to 10k cumulative followers.
- Position as go-to expert on blending Adobe + AI + front-end development.

### Core KPIs
| Funnel Stage | KPI | Targets | Data Sources |
|--------------|-----|---------|--------------|
| Awareness | Reach, impressions, follower growth rate | +8% MoM per channel | Platform analytics, social listening |
| Engagement | Engagement rate, saves, shares, comments | 6–8% on IG reels/carousels; 5% LinkedIn | Native analytics, Metricool/Later |
| Conversion | Portfolio clicks, inquiry form completions, newsletter signups | 10 weekly qualified leads | GA4 (UTM links), CRM, Notion dashboard |
| Retention | Repeat client touchpoints, NPS, referral volume | 2 referral projects per quarter | CRM notes, surveys |

### Supporting Data to Monitor
- Content format performance (reel vs carousel vs case study).
- Audience demographic shifts and active time slots.
- AI-related keyword trends, community sentiment.
- Portfolio page heatmaps and scroll depth (via Hotjar/Clarity).

## 4. Messaging Pillars & Story Arcs
1. **Design Process Transparency** – sketch-to-shipping breakdowns, time-lapses, Figma to After Effects journeys.
2. **AI-Accelerated Creativity** – tutorials on Photoshop Beta, Firefly, Runway ML, Cursor/Claude for code scaffolding.
3. **Interactive Web Experiences** – highlight animations, motion design, and performance metrics (Lighthouse scores, accessibility wins).
4. **Client Success & Social Proof** – testimonials, before/after redesigns, ROI snapshots.
5. **Personal Brand & Workflow** – workspace tours, tool stack rundowns, creative rituals.

Create a messaging matrix that pairs each persona with relevant pillars, preferred formats, and CTAs (e.g., "Book a discovery call", "Download the AI-ready brief template").

## 6. 90-Day Roadmap
### Phase 1 – Foundation (Weeks 1–3)
- Audit all existing channels; benchmark competitors and hashtags.
- Finalize personas, value prop, and tone of voice document.
- Build Notion/Airtable calendar with status, owner, CTA, asset requirements.
- Design reusable templates: IG carousel, LinkedIn carousel, Behance case study, YouTube thumbnail.
- Produce asset kit: logo variations, color palette, typography guide, branded lower-thirds.

### Phase 2 – Launch (Weeks 4–8)
- Kick off hero campaign: **"From Concept to Code"** spotlighting AI-assisted workflows.
- Release weekly reels/TikToks showing real client work (with permissions) or internal projects.
- Publish LinkedIn article featuring metrics from a recent redesign (conversion lift, load speed).
- Activate two micro-influencers (design educators, indie devs) for co-created content.
- Deploy paid boosts on top-performing Instagram reel and LinkedIn carousel to targeted audiences.

### Phase 3 – Optimization & Expansion (Weeks 9–12)
- Run A/B tests on hooks, cover frames, CTA placements.
- Convert best-performing short-form video into a YouTube deep dive and downloadable guide.
- Launch UGC/Community initiative: hashtag challenge inviting peers to remix your design-to-dev workflow.
- Host monthly live session (IG Live or LinkedIn Live) answering design + AI + dev questions.
- Review analytics, refine service landing pages, and refresh ad audiences based on engaged users.

## 8. Creative Asset & Production Checklist
### Graphics & Static Assets
- Brand style guide (color, type, logo usage).
- Social media cover banners, profile image variants, carousel templates (InDesign/Figma).
- Icon/illustration set reflecting AI + development themes.
- Data visualization pack (bar/line charts) for sharing project metrics.

### Motion & Video Assets
- Intro/outro animations for Reels, TikTok, YouTube.
- Screen-recorded walkthroughs with callout graphics.
- B-roll library: workspace shots, sketching, coding, client calls.
- Testimonial videos edited for vertical (9:16) and horizontal (16:9).
- Template for subtitles and motion graphics (After Effects or Premiere presets).

### Copy & Script Resources
- Hook bank segmented by platform (e.g., "I redesigned this in 3 hours using Firefly").
- CTA list aligned to funnel stages.
- Caption frameworks (Hook → Value → Proof → CTA) stored in Notion.
- FAQ/objection handling sheet for community managers.

## 9. Marketing Funnel Alignment
1. **Awareness:** Trend-aligned reels, TikToks, YouTube Shorts featuring quick wins and visuals.
2. **Engagement:** Tutorials, carousels, live streams, Story polls gathering audience input.
3. **Conversion:** Portfolio showcases, client case studies with metrics, lead magnets ("AI-ready project brief").
4. **Retention:** Monthly newsletter, invite-only Discord/Slack or Notion resource hub, post-project feedback loops.

Ensure all assets include UTM-tagged links and consistent CTAs to guide prospects through the funnel.

## 10. Measurement & Reporting Rhythm
- **Weekly Pulse:** Track top 3 performing posts, audience growth, website sessions, pending inquiries.
- **Monthly Review:** Analyze format performance, paid vs organic results, AI-related content resonance; adjust pillars accordingly.
- **Quarterly Deep Dive:** Evaluate lead quality, revenue attribution, service mix; sunset underperforming channels.

**Tools:** GA4, Adobe Analytics (if available), Metricool/Later dashboards, Notion analytics tracker, CRM (HubSpot, Trello, or Airtable). Layer in social listening (Brandwatch/Sprout) for sentiment and share-of-voice.

## 11. Workflow, Automation & Governance
- **Team Roles/RACI:** Content strategist (you), copywriter/AI assistant, video editor, community manager (can be you + automation), paid media specialist (contract as needed).
- **Automation:** Use Zapier/Make to push new inquiries to Notion/Slack, schedule reminders, or repurpose threads into newsletters.
- **Scheduling:** Buffer, Loomly, or Later with approval queues; maintain asset naming conventions in cloud storage.
- **Contingency:** Crisis response template, evergreen content bank for gaps, documented process for algorithm updates.

## 12. Community & Amplification
- Launch ambassador/affiliate program with incentives (template packs, referral commission).
- Collaborate with design/dev podcasters or YouTubers for guest appearances.
- Encourage UGC via monthly prompts (e.g., "Show us your AI-generated mood board").
- Repurpose client testimonials into quote graphics and vertical video snippets.
- Cross-promote with complementary vendors (copywriters, SEO specialists, 3D artists).

## 13. Paid Media Integration
- Test $100–$150/mo on Instagram and LinkedIn to amplify hero assets targeting lookalike audiences.
- Retarget site visitors with carousel ads featuring service packages and testimonials.
- Run seasonal offers (e.g., "Holiday Landing Page Sprint") with countdown creatives.
- Track ROAS and cost-per-lead; pivot budget towards formats with highest conversion.

By executing this integrated plan—and continually iterating based on performance data—you will amplify your hybrid designer–developer brand, showcase your AI-enhanced workflows, and convert engaged followers into long-term clients.
